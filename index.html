Western Test Systems, Inc. began in 1993 as a supplier of refurbished microwave test components and electronic test equipment. Our customers include:
  • Satellite communications and aerospace designers
  • Semiconductor manufacturers
  • Wireless and Internet connectivity designers
  • Businesses, colleges, and universities worldwide
Our mission is to provide equipment which is verified to meet manufacturers' original specifications. We hope you find the item you are looking for on our website. However, the items offered online are only a partial listing of the inventory we have on hand in our warehouse.
